It began with an alien appearing before me, which, of course, I did not know at the time. A small gray being stood before me in the dark, which I could hardly make out other than the shine of His large black eyes and its outstretched hand as it approached me. I reached out my hand and touched His, my flesh connecting with its cold clammy fingertip as it began.


Earlier in the day I had found myself doing my usual research in a new location, where others had reportedly noticed suspicious activities. I myself went to investigate to confirm such findings. With this, I noticed a flash in the distance and followed the light to investigate further. At this point it was nearly dark, just around civil twilight. As I observed the positioning of various stars and celestial bodies to take my measurements, something strange occurred. My compass began to malfunction, turning back and forth instead of steadily at north. My flashlight began to flicker wildly, before eventually burning out. The sun fully set as I turned to head back. I lost my path within the woods without my flashlight or compass to guide me. That is when I saw it.


What I saw cannot be fully conveyed by the English language. First came darkness. It felt like hours. Of pure nothing. No light, no sound, and no feeling– pure sensory deprivation. Then a tiny light came out of the darkness. One small dot, much like a star, which slowly but surely expanded until it filled my entire field of vision. Then there were colors, those which do not have names, those beyond the human spectrum. Finally, images appeared, flashing before my eyes.
