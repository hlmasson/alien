First, images of a demolished world. Our world. Earth obliterated, our every human structure in absolute ruin. The environment utterly destroyed with every bit of green gone, all rivers dried to dust, every beautiful wonder of the world completely gone. Not one human being there to be seen, only the bones left of a civilization destroyed. Next came the visions of war. Horrifying explosions, technology the likes of which we have never even conceptualized, and gruesome images of death all laid out in front of me.


Finally the extraterrestrials. The grays, much like the one I had seen before, organized together in their colonies. Hundreds of thousands of them in a space I cannot accurately describe. The technology was nothing like anything I have ever seen. Then I saw another type of being. I had never considered the idea that there were multiple, and much less that they were working together. Much like the grays, they had large black eyes and thin gangly limbs, but this kind was twice as tall with pale skin, flesh so light it seemed to glow a bright white. The way the move looked almost as if they floated, so regal and elegant as they commanded their lower race. These creatures are clearly the Highest beings of all else.
